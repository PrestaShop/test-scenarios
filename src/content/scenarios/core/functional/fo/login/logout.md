# Logout
## Steps
| Step Description | Expected result |
| ----- | ----- |
| On the FO > Customer account > Click on top right corner "Sign out" button | You are logged out<br><br>Log in to your account page is displayed |
| Log into your account with email + password > Click "Sign in" button | You are logged in<br><br>Your account page is displayed |
| Click on "Sign out" link on the bottom of your account page | You are logged out<br><br>Log in to your account page is displayed |
